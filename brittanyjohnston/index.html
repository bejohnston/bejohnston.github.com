Beyond being a designer, I put a lot of energy into everything I do.

I've been creating for as far back as I can remember and has been an essential part of my life. Being a designer enables me to continue doing what I love while expanding upon my skills.

Currently, I'm a junior Graphic Design major at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I immense myself in all my classes and like to learn as much as I can from the world around me to improve my work. I draw much of my inspiration from my illustrations and other doodles I make in my sketchbooks but still maintain a high standard for making clean and effective designs. I also have a huge obsession for hand-lettering, it usually makes things like my to-do lists look much more aesthetically pleasing in the end. It has always been one of my beliefs that Graphic Design and Illustration go hand-in-hand and for that reason I practice working both with computer and hand-drawn formats.

I originally hail from a small farm town in North Stonington, CT but currently making it as a city girl in Boston, MA. When I'm not putting all my time and energy into one of my projects, you can usually find me spending time with my friends, exploring what the city has to offer, or playing my guitar.
