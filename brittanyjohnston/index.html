Even beyond being a designer, I put a lot of energy into everything I do.

I've been been in the business of creating as far back as I can remember and has been an essential part of my life. Being a designer to means being able to continue doing what I love while expanding upon my skills.

Currently, I'm a junior Graphic Design major at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I'm immense myself in all my classes and like to learn as much as I can from the world around me to improve my work. I draw much of my inspiration from my illustrations and other doodles I make in my sketchbooks but still like to maintain a high standard for clean and effective design. It has always been one of my beliefs that Graphic Design and Illustration go hand-in-hand and for that reason I practice both regularly.

I hail from a small farm town in North Stonington, CT but currently making it as a city girl in Boston, MA. When I'm not putting all my time and energy into one of my projects, you can usually find me surrounded by good company, exploring the sights of the city, or playing my guitar. I also have a huge obsession with making hand-lettering, it usually makes all my to-do lists look much more aesthetically pleasing in the end.
