Let me tell you more about me.

I put a lot of energy into everything I do, always have, always will. I've been creating as for as long as I can remember and ever since then have made it essential to my everyday life. Currently, I'm a junior graphic design major at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I'm highly involved in all my classes and like learn as much as I can from the world around me to improve my work. I often draw inspiration from my illustrations and other doodles I make in my sketchbooks. It is one of my beliefs that illustration and graphic design go hand-in-hand and for that reason I practice both quite frequently!

When I'm not going crazy working on new projects, you can usually find me monkeying around with my friends, catching up on the latest T.V. show, going for a run or playing my guitar.
