Letter "E" 8 1/2" x 11"

This letterform I created is part of a 26-letter alphabet type journal I made to demonstrate various types of letterforms. I have always found the letter "E" in uppercase form to be too stiff, and therefore tried to think how it could appear to be more light and airy. Feathers in fun shades of purple just seemed like the perfect solution.
