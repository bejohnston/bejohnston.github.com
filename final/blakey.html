Blake 8 1/2" x 11"

An illustration I did out of pure enjoyment for the show Workaholics. Blake is my all-time favorite character on the show and I thought he deserved to be drawn. I was also able to capture his personality the best through the type of elements I used, such as the thick and thin line weights that depict a hand-drawn feel and show-case his goofy self.
