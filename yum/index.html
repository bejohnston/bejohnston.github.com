• cooking spray
  • 1 1/3 cups fat-free vanilla wafter cookie crumbs
  • ¼ unsalted butter
  • 3 (8 oz) packages fat-free cream cheese
  • 1 cup fat-free sour cream
  • 2 ½ cups sugar or sugar substitute
  • 4 large eggs or ¾ cup egg substitute
  • 3 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 table spoons unsalted butter cut into bits

Almost Fat-Free Lemon Cheesecake

Prep

Pre-heat oven to 325º F. Lightly spray (9”) springfoam pan with cooking spray.

Make

Mix crumbs and butter in melted bowl. Press crumb mixture onto bottum and 1 ½ inches up side of pan. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are lightly golden. Cool on rack.
With electric mixer on medium-high beat cream cheese and sour cream for 2 to 3 minutesuntil fluffy. Gradually beat in sugar or sugar substitute. Beat in eggs one at a time. Beat in zest and juice. Pour into. Bake one hour or until center is almost set. Let cool completely.

Serve

Run thin blade around edges of springfoam pan and remove sides. Transfer to serving plate. Spread lemon curd over top. Let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes. Cool cheescake in refrigerator several hours or overnight before serving.

Best Paired With: The Notebook

Tips:

Garnish with zest and raspberries if desired
Do not overbake, as it will firm as it cools
